Common Questions

What are the requirements to rent an exotic car?

To rent a vehicle from Primo Rentals, you must be at least 25 years old, possess a valid driver's license, and have proof of insurance. A credit card is also required for the security deposit.

Do I need insurance to rent a car?

Yes, we require all renters to have full coverage insurance. If you don't have it, we can help you arrange temporary coverage for the duration of the rental.

Can I rent a car for a single day?

Yes, at Primo Rentals, we offer flexible rental options, including daily, weekly, and even hourly rentals, so you can enjoy the car on your terms.

What types of cars are available?

We have a wide range of exotic cars available, from high-end sports cars like Ferraris and Lamborghinis to luxurious SUVs like Range Rovers and Bentleys.

Is there a mileage limit on rentals?

Each rental comes with a set mileage allowance. Additional miles can be purchased if needed. The specific limit depends on the vehicle you choose.

Can I have the car delivered to my location?

Yes, we offer delivery and pickup services for your convenience. Whether you're at home, the airport, or a hotel, we can arrange delivery anywhere within our service area.

What happens if the car gets damaged during my rental?

If any damage occurs, you will be responsible for the repair costs, as per the rental agreement. We recommend thoroughly reviewing your insurance coverage before renting.

How do I make a reservation?

You can easily make a reservation through our website or by calling our customer service team. A deposit is required to confirm your booking.
