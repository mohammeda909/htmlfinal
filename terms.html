General Terms

Rental Agreement

By renting a vehicle from Primo Rentals, you agree to all terms and conditions laid out in this document. A rental agreement must be signed before taking possession of the vehicle, which outlines specific terms based on the vehicle and rental period.

Age and License Requirements

All renters must be at least 25 years of age and hold a valid, government-issued driver's license. The license must be valid for the duration of the rental period.

Insurance

Full insurance coverage is required for all rentals. Renters must provide proof of insurance or purchase supplemental coverage through Primo Rentals. Failure to provide adequate insurance may result in the cancellation of the rental.

Security Deposit

A refundable security deposit is required to cover any potential damage to the vehicle. The deposit amount varies depending on the type of vehicle and will be refunded upon the safe return of the car.

Rental Use

Vehicle Usage

The rented vehicle must only be used for personal use. The renter is prohibited from using the vehicle for any commercial activity, racing, towing, or off-road driving. Violations will result in additional charges and may void the rental agreement.

Mileage Allowance

Each rental includes a mileage allowance. Additional mileage is subject to extra charges, as outlined in the rental agreement. Please inquire about specific vehicle allowances at the time of rental.

Fuel Policy

Vehicles are provided with a full tank of gas and must be returned with a full tank. Failure to refuel the vehicle will result in refueling charges.

Payment and Cancellation

Payment Terms

Full payment for the rental is due at the time of booking. We accept all major credit cards. The security deposit will be authorized at the time of vehicle pickup.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made at least 48 hours before the rental date will receive a full refund. Cancellations made less than 48 hours in advance will incur a cancellation fee of 50% of the rental cost.

Vehicle Condition and Return

Vehicle Inspection

All vehicles are inspected before and after the rental period. Renters are responsible for any damage that occurs during the rental period. Any damage or excessive wear and tear may result in additional charges.

Late Return

Vehicles must be returned by the agreed-upon time. Late returns will incur additional fees, as outlined in the rental agreement. Please inform us ahead of time if a late return is anticipated.
